What's coming here

This page will grow into a complete "build your own" hub. The plan, roughly in order:

🧮

Parts configurator

Pick your hive count (up to 18) and sensors and get a tailored bill of materials — quantities, modules and buy links — matched to your build.

🔧

Assembly tutorials

Step-by-step guides for the Scale Module and Power Module: module placement, load cells, I2C sensors, wiring and the enclosure.

⚡

Flashing & setup

From PlatformIO upload to the on-device provisioning portal, sensor pairing and claiming the device in HivePal.

🔌

Wiring diagrams

Per-sensor wiring and pin maps generated to match the exact build you configured — no guessing from a generic table.

Have ideas for what the builder should cover? Open an issue on GitHub.
